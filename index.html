Who We Are

A future for the sport in Arkansas

Arkansas State USBC is the official governing body for sanctioned bowling across Arkansas, chartered under USBC national. We provide standardized rules, conduct state championship tournaments for youth, adults and seniors, recognize achievement through awards and our Hall of Fame, and support the local associations and centers that grow the game.

Our work is carried out by a volunteer board of directors and committees, and funded through membership and tournament participation. We welcome every bowler — from first-time youth leaguers to lifelong competitors.

SafeSport & RVP Requirements

SafeSport Training

USBC requires all adults in a position of authority over minor athletes — coaches, officials, chaperones, and volunteers working with youth — to complete SafeSport Awareness training before participating in any youth event. Training is free and takes approximately 90 minutes online.

Complete SafeSport Training ↗
RVP Background Check

USBC's Registered Volunteer Program (RVP) requires all adults who interact with youth bowlers in an official capacity to complete a national background check. This is mandatory for certified coaches, team chaperones, and tournament volunteers serving youth programs in Arkansas.
